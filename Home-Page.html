BNFOthon 2025

The VCU Center for Biological Data Science proudly announces BNFOthon 2025—a hackathon devoted to solving bioinformatics problems by combining knowledge and skills from biology, medicine, statistics, and computer sciences. 

BNFOthon brings together people from all academic levels, majors, and career paths to solve multidisciplinary problems through informatic means.

BNFOthon is a 24-hr hackathon event run by students for students. We aim to provide a collaborative environment for innovative thinkers and invite students, mentors, and sponsors to network as they learn about the field of bioinformatics. 

Participants are encouraged to work in teams of up to five members, and all departments and programs at VCU are welcome to attend!

This year’s BNFOthon will be held on April 5-6th in Harris Hall and will be a hybrid in-person/online event. 

Expect 24 hours of fun, crafting solutions to Bioinformatics-based prompts. Food will be provided, along with entertainment, workshops, swag, and more! No coding experience required. The winning teams will receive grand prizes.
