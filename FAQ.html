FAQ

Where is BNFOthon 2025 taking place?
Most of the rooms reserved for hacking are in Harris Hall. The list of rooms will be made available on the BNFOthon 2025 discord server.Where do I go for the start of the event?Details will be given at the start of the event.

Do I need previous programming experience to participate in BNFOthon?
No! This hackathon is meant to be accessible to hackers of all skill levels. We will have mentors available to help work through our challenges and prompts. Some prompts will require very little coding and will be friendly to those with a biological/design background. However, we hope through collaboration, everyone can learn something from everyone! Workshops will also be provided where new skills can be learned.
